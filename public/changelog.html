June 2026
In-session intervention — the wedge
  • New In-Session Intervention — Kōda now intercepts you when you tap Log Trade after 2+ losses, near your daily limit, in a revenge window, or with a tilt-emotion tag on your last trade. 5 signals, configurable cooldown, no broker API required
  • New Discipline Settings — toggle the feature on/off and pick your cooldown duration (Off / 5 / 15 / 30 min)
  • New In-Session Check-Ins card — Stats tab shows your 7-day rollup of fired / continued / cancelled events
  • New Ideas section — pre-trade setups + post-trade breakdowns. Public chronological feed, like-only social, optional chart + linked trade
  • New News tab — economic calendar (ForexFactory) + headlines (Marketaux). High + medium impact only, timezone picker, USD / all-FX toggle
  • Improved Stats tab — new "Computed · not LLM-guessed" badge under the title. Every number Kōda shows is from your logged trades. No model. No guess.
  • Improved Discipline toggle now matches the dark-mode pill-switch instead of a native checkbox
  • Improved Paywall lead feature reordered — in-session intervention is now the first thing on the upgrade screen
  • Improved Landing page now has real indexable content for search engines and link unfurlers (X, Slack, iMessage)
  • Improved OG image upgraded from SVG to PNG with summary_large_image card — links now unfurl as full preview cards
  • Fix registerSW.js 404 — vite-plugin-pwa was injecting a redundant script tag on every page
  • Fix 268 latent TypeScript errors cleaned up — the typecheck command was silently passing against the wrong tsconfig
  • Fix Sentry was reporting [object Object] for any non-Error throw — now extracts the real message
  • Fix Broker-sync cron disabled — was hitting Tradovate every 5 min with no provisioned credentials
