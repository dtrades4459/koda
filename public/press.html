Boilerplate

Short (1 sentence) — Kōda is the trading journal that intercepts you before a bad trade, not after.

Standard (3 sentences) — Kōda is the trading journal built for traders who want to stop self-sabotaging. By watching five behavioural signals from your own logged activity — consecutive losses, daily loss-limit %, trade cap, tilt tags, and rapid log-button taps — Kōda steps in mid-session with a calm intervention sheet before the over-trade happens. It's a journal that talks back, available as a Progressive Web App on iOS, Android, and desktop.

Brand rules

Two short rules.

Do — write "Kōda" with the macron (Ō). When the macron isn't typable, write "Koda".
Don't — write "KODA", "koda", "Kohda", or "Kōda Trading". The product is "Kōda" or "Kōda OS".
Do — pair the wordmark with the live mint accent (oklch(0.84 0.14 175)) on dark backgrounds, or with charcoal on light backgrounds.
Don't — recolour the wordmark, stretch it, rotate it, or place it on busy backgrounds without a solid panel beneath.
